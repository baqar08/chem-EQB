About chemEQB

chem-EQB is a multipurpose chemistry helper tool. Its main use is to automate complex calculations, saving you time and helping you verify your work, whether you are a student or a professional.

Molar Mass Calculator

Calculates the total molar mass (or molecular weight) of any chemical compound.

Example:
Input: C6H12O6

Equation Balancer

Finds the correct stoichiometric coefficients to balance a chemical reaction.

Example:
Input: H2 + O2 -> H2O

Mass Composition

Finds the mass percentage of each element within a single compound.

Example:
Input: H2O
